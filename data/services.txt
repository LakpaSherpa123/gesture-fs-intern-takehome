Starter marketing agency - Services

SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGEMENT
We manage your brand's presence across Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, X, and Facebook. Our team creates and schedules 12-20 posts per month per platform, handles community management, and responds to comments and DMs within 4 hours during business hours. Monthly analytics reports are included with all social media packages. We also run paid social campaigns with a minimum ad spend of $500 per platform per month. Content includes static graphics, short-form video (Reels/TikTok), carousels, and Stories.

SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMIZATION (SEO)
Our SEO service includes technical site audits, keyword research, on-page optimization, and monthly content creation. We publish 4-8 SEO-optimized blog posts per month depending on your package. Link building is included in our Growth and Enterprise tiers. We track rankings for up to 50 keywords on the Starter plan, 150 on Growth, and unlimited on Enterprise. Results typically begin showing within 3-6 months. We do not guarantee specific ranking positions but we do guarantee increased organic traffic within 6 months or we extend the contract at no cost.

PAID ADVERTISING (PPC)
We manage Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, and TikTok Ads campaigns. Setup includes audience research, ad creative design, landing page recommendations, and conversion tracking. Our management fee is 15% of monthly ad spend with a minimum spend of $2,000 per month. We provide weekly performance reports and bi-weekly strategy calls. A/B testing is standard across all campaigns. Average client sees 3-5x return on ad spend within the first 90 days.

BRANDING AND DESIGN
Our branding packages include logo design, brand guidelines, color palette, typography selection, and brand voice documentation. A full rebrand takes 4-6 weeks and includes 3 rounds of revisions. We also offer ongoing design support for social media graphics, email templates, presentation decks, and print materials at $120 per hour or through a monthly retainer. All deliverables include full commercial usage rights.

EMAIL MARKETING
We build and manage email campaigns using Klaviyo, Mailchimp, or HubSpot. Services include list segmentation, automated flows (welcome series, abandoned cart, re-engagement), campaign design, copywriting, and A/B testing. We send up to 8 campaigns per month on the Starter plan and unlimited on Growth and Enterprise. Average open rates across our clients are 28% and click-through rates are 3.5%. Setup of automation flows takes 2-3 weeks.

CONTENT CREATION
Our in-house team produces blog posts, whitepapers, case studies, video scripts, podcast outlines, and social media copy. Blog posts start at $250 for 800-1200 words. Whitepapers and ebooks start at $1,500. We also produce short-form video content for TikTok and Reels starting at $500 per video including scripting, filming, and editing. All content goes through a two-round review process with the client before publishing.
