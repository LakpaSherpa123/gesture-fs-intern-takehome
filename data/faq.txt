Starter marketing agency - Client FAQ

HOW DOES ONBOARDING WORK?
Once you sign the contract, your onboarding kicks off within 48 hours. We schedule a 90-minute kickoff call to understand your business, target audience, competitors, and goals. Within the first week we conduct a full audit of your current marketing channels. By week two you will receive a 90-day strategy document for review and approval. Content creation and campaign launches begin in week three. The full onboarding process takes about 3 weeks.

HOW LONG UNTIL I SEE RESULTS?
Paid advertising campaigns typically show initial results within 2-4 weeks and reach optimal performance within 60-90 days. SEO is a longer game and organic traffic improvements usually appear within 3-6 months. Social media growth depends on your starting point but most clients see 20-40% follower growth in the first 6 months. Email marketing results are fastest with most clients seeing revenue from email within the first month of launching automation flows.

CAN I CANCEL MY CONTRACT?
Yes. After your minimum commitment period you can cancel with 30 days written notice. Early termination before the minimum commitment requires payment of 50% of the remaining contract value. We offer a satisfaction guarantee in the first 30 days where if you are not happy with the strategy we will revise it at no additional cost.

WHO WILL I WORK WITH?
Every client is assigned an account manager who is your main point of contact. Depending on your package you may also work directly with a content strategist, designer, and paid media specialist. Enterprise clients get a dedicated team of 3-4 people. You will always know who is working on your account and can reach your account manager via email or Slack.

DO YOU WORK WITH COMPETITORS?
We do not work with direct competitors in the same market. Before signing any new client we check for conflicts. If a conflict exists we will be transparent about it and work with both parties to find a solution. In most cases we maintain a policy of only one client per industry per geographic market.

WHAT TOOLS DO YOU USE?
We use industry-standard tools including Semrush for SEO, Sprout Social for social media management, Google Analytics and Looker Studio for reporting, Figma for design, Klaviyo and Mailchimp for email, and Google Ads and Meta Business Suite for paid advertising. Clients get view access to all relevant dashboards. We are also experienced with HubSpot, Salesforce, and Shopify integrations.

DO YOU OFFER ONE-TIME PROJECTS?
Yes. Outside of our monthly packages we take on one-time projects such as full rebrands, website redesigns, product launch campaigns, and event marketing. One-time projects are scoped and quoted individually. Typical turnaround is 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. A 50% deposit is required upfront with the balance due on delivery.
